Inzamam-ul-Haq

3 March 1970 (age 50 years), Multan, Pakistan

Test debut (cap 124): 4 June 1992 v England

Spouse: Kashifa ul-Haq

Syed Inzamam-ul-Haq, also known as Inzi, is a Pakistani cricket coach and former Pakistan cricketer. He was the leading run scorer for Pakistan in one-day internationals, and the third-highest run scorer for Pakistan in Test cricket. He is the only Pakistani batsman to score 20,000 runs in international cricket arena.
